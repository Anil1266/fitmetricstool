What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using a person’s height and weight. It is commonly used to classify individuals as underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

BMI Formula:

BMI = Weight (kg) / Height (m²)

BMI Categories for Indian Adults

  • Below 18.5 – Underweight
  • 18.5 – 22.9 – Normal weight
  • 23 – 24.9 – Overweight
  • 25 and above – Obese

Indian BMI standards are slightly stricter because health risks such as diabetes and heart disease may occur at lower BMI levels in Asian populations.

Why Maintaining a Healthy BMI is Important

Maintaining a healthy BMI can reduce the risk of several lifestyle diseases including:

  • Heart disease
  • High blood pressure
  • Type 2 diabetes
  • Joint problems

Healthy Weight Tips

Maintaining a healthy body weight requires balanced nutrition, regular exercise, adequate sleep, and stress management. Small consistent lifestyle improvements can make a big difference.
