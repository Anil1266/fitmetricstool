Healthy Weight Tips for Indian Adults

Maintaining a healthy body weight is important for long-term health and well-being. Here are practical tips to help Indian adults stay fit and active.

1. Balanced Diet

Include a mix of proteins, healthy fats, and complex carbohydrates in your meals. Reduce excessive sugar and processed food intake.

2. Regular Physical Activity

Aim for at least 30 minutes of exercise daily. Walking, cycling, yoga, and strength training are excellent options.

3. Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water helps control hunger and improves metabolism.
