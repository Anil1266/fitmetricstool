Healthy Weight Tips for Indian Adults

Maintaining a healthy body weight requires balanced nutrition, regular physical activity, and proper lifestyle habits.

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Include proteins, complex carbohydrates, vegetables, and healthy fats in your meals. Avoid excessive sugar and processed foods.

2. Exercise Regularly

Aim for at least 30 minutes of physical activity daily such as walking, yoga, cycling, or strength training.

3. Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water helps improve metabolism and control hunger.

4. Sleep Properly

Getting 7–8 hours of quality sleep supports healthy weight management.
