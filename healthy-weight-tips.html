Healthy Weight Tips

1. Balanced Diet

Eat protein, vegetables, complex carbohydrates and healthy fats.

2. Exercise Daily

Aim for at least 30 minutes of physical activity.

3. Sleep Well

7–8 hours of sleep helps maintain healthy weight.

4. Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water improves metabolism.
