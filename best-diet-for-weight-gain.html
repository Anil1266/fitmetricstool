Best Diet for Healthy Weight Gain

Gaining weight in a healthy way requires consuming more calories than your body burns while focusing on nutrient-dense foods.

Healthy Foods for Weight Gain

  • Whole grains
  • Lean proteins
  • Nuts and seeds
  • Healthy fats
  • Dairy products

Tips for Weight Gain

  • Eat frequent meals
  • Add calorie-dense foods
  • Include protein in each meal
  • Strength training exercises
