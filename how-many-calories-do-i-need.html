How Many Calories Should I Eat Per Day?

Calories are a measure of energy that your body uses to function properly. Understanding how many calories you need each day can help you maintain a healthy weight, lose fat, or build muscle.

What Are Calories?

Calories represent the amount of energy your body gets from food and drinks. Your body uses this energy for breathing, digestion, movement, and exercise.

Average Daily Calorie Needs

The number of calories you need depends on your age, gender, height, weight, and activity level.

Average daily calorie needs:

  • Adult women: 1,800 – 2,200 calories
  • Adult men: 2,200 – 2,800 calories

Calories for Weight Loss

To lose weight, you need to consume fewer calories than your body burns. A safe calorie deficit is usually about 500 calories per day.

Calories for Muscle Gain

To build muscle, your body needs extra energy. This means eating slightly more calories while combining it with strength training.

Use a Calorie Calculator

Instead of guessing your calorie needs, you can use a calculator to estimate your daily calorie requirements.

Final Thoughts

Knowing your daily calorie needs can help you make better decisions about diet and exercise. Combine healthy eating with regular physical activity to maintain a balanced lifestyle.
