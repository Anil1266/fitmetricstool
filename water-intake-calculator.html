Water Intake Calculator

Why Daily Water Intake is Important

Water plays a vital role in maintaining body temperature, lubricating joints, transporting nutrients, and removing waste products. Staying properly hydrated improves energy levels, concentration, and overall health.

How Much Water Should You Drink?

This calculator estimates your daily water needs based on body weight. A common recommendation is approximately 30–35 ml of water per kilogram of body weight.

Benefits of Proper Hydration

  • Improved digestion
  • Better skin health
  • Increased energy levels
  • Enhanced physical performance
  • Reduced headaches and fatigue

Factors That Increase Water Needs

  • Hot climate
  • Exercise
  • Pregnancy
  • Illness
  • High-protein diet

Frequently Asked Questions

Can drinking too much water be harmful?

Yes, excessive water intake can lead to water intoxication. Drink according to your body’s needs.

Does tea and coffee count as water?

Yes, but plain water is still the healthiest option for hydration.
