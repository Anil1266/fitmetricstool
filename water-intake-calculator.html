What is Water Intake Calculator?

This calculator estimates the amount of water a person should drink daily based on body weight.

Why Drinking Water is Important

  • Supports metabolism
  • Helps digestion
  • Maintains energy levels
  • Regulates body temperature

How Water Intake is Estimated

A common guideline recommends about 30–35 ml of water per kilogram of body weight per day.
