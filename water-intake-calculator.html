Water Intake Calculator

What is Water Intake Calculator?

This calculator estimates how much water you should drink daily based on your body weight. Proper hydration is essential for maintaining body functions and overall health.

Benefits of Drinking Enough Water

  • Improves metabolism
  • Supports digestion
  • Maintains energy levels
  • Helps regulate body temperature

How Water Intake is Estimated

A common guideline is around 30–35 ml of water per kilogram of body weight per day. This calculator uses your weight to estimate recommended intake.
