About FitMetrics Tools

FitMetrics Tools is a free online platform designed to help people better understand their health and fitness metrics.

Our website provides simple and easy-to-use calculators that help users estimate important health indicators such as:

  • Body Mass Index (BMI)
  • Daily Calorie Needs
  • Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)
  • Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE)
  • Daily Water Intake
  • Ideal Body Weight

These tools are intended for informational purposes only and should not replace professional medical advice.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make health information more accessible by providing simple calculators and educational resources that help people make better lifestyle choices.
