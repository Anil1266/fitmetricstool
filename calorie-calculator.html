Daily Calorie Calculator

What is a Calorie Calculator?

A calorie calculator estimates how many calories your body needs daily to maintain, lose, or gain weight. It uses your age, gender, weight, height, and activity level to calculate your daily energy requirements.

How Daily Calories Are Calculated

This calculator first estimates your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), which is the number of calories your body burns at rest. It then multiplies your BMR by an activity factor to determine your total daily calorie needs.

Why Knowing Your Calorie Needs is Important

Understanding your daily calorie requirement helps you:

  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Plan balanced meals
  • Support muscle gain
  • Reduce body fat safely
  • Improve overall health

Calories for Weight Loss

To lose weight, you generally need to consume fewer calories than your body burns. A safe calorie deficit is usually 300–500 calories per day.

Calories for Muscle Gain

For healthy weight gain, increase your calorie intake gradually while combining it with strength training exercises.

Factors That Affect Calorie Needs

  • Age
  • Gender
  • Activity level
  • Muscle mass
  • Metabolism

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is a calorie calculator?

Calorie calculators provide an estimate. Individual metabolism may vary.

Should I eat exactly the number shown?

Use the number as a guideline and adjust based on your progress and health goals.
