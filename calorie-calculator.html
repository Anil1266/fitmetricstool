What is a Calorie Calculator?

A calorie calculator estimates the number of calories your body needs each day based on factors like age, height, weight, and activity level.

Why Use a Calorie Calculator?

  • Helps manage body weight
  • Supports diet planning
  • Useful for fitness goals
  • Helps understand daily energy needs

Who Should Use This?

This calculator is useful for anyone who wants to monitor their health, track fitness progress, and maintain a balanced lifestyle.

How Calories Are Calculated

The calculator estimates your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and multiplies it by your activity level to determine daily calorie needs.
