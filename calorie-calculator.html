What is a Calorie Calculator?

A calorie calculator estimates how many calories your body needs each day based on your age, weight, height, and activity level.

Why Use It?

  • Manage weight effectively
  • Plan diet properly
  • Support fitness goals
  • Understand daily energy needs

How It Works

The calculator estimates your BMR and adjusts it based on your activity level to determine total daily calorie needs.
