What is a Calorie Calculator?

A calorie calculator estimates how many calories your body needs daily based on age, gender, weight, height and activity level.

Why Calories Matter

  • Calories provide energy for your body.
  • Weight loss requires a calorie deficit.
  • Muscle gain usually requires a calorie surplus.
  • Balanced calorie intake supports overall health.
