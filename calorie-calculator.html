How to Use the Calorie Calculator

This calorie calculator estimates the number of calories your body needs each day based on your age, gender, height, weight, and activity level.

Steps to Calculate Your Daily Calories

  • Enter your age
  • Select your gender
  • Enter your weight and height
  • Choose your activity level
  • Click the calculate button

Why Calorie Intake Matters

Calories are the energy your body uses to perform daily functions. Maintaining the right calorie balance helps with weight management, muscle gain, and overall health.

General Calorie Guidelines

  • Adult women: 1,800 – 2,200 calories/day
  • Adult men: 2,200 – 2,800 calories/day

What Is a Calorie Calculator?

A calorie calculator estimates the number of calories your body needs each day based on factors like age, gender, height, weight, and physical activity.

Understanding your daily calorie needs helps you manage your weight, maintain energy levels, and achieve fitness goals.

Why Daily Calories Matter

  • Helps maintain healthy body weight
  • Supports muscle growth
  • Provides energy for daily activities
  • Helps plan balanced nutrition

Frequently Asked Questions

How many calories should I eat per day?

Daily calorie needs vary depending on age, body size, and activity level. Most adults need between 1,800 and 2,800 calories per day.

How do I lose weight using calories?

Weight loss typically requires consuming fewer calories than your body burns, creating a calorie deficit.

Is calorie counting necessary?

Calorie counting can help some people understand their eating habits and manage their weight effectively.
