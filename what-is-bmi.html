What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a health measurement that compares your weight to your height.

Formula: BMI = Weight (kg) / Height (m²)

It helps classify individuals into weight categories and identify possible health risks.

BMI Categories (Indian Standard)

  • Below 18.5 – Underweight
  • 18.5 – 22.9 – Normal
  • 23 – 24.9 – Overweight
  • 25+ – Obese
