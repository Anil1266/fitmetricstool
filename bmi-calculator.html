BMI Calculator

Calculate your Body Mass Index using your weight and height.

What is BMI Calculator?

The BMI calculator estimates Body Mass Index using height and weight. It helps determine whether a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese based on health guidelines.

Why Use a BMI Calculator?

  • Helps understand body weight category
  • Supports weight management goals
  • Provides quick health estimation
  • Widely used in health assessments

How BMI is Calculated

BMI is calculated using the formula:

BMI = Weight (kg) / Height² (meters)

Enter your height and weight and the calculator will compute your BMI instantly.
