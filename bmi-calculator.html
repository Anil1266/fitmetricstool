BMI Calculator

Calculate your Body Mass Index instantly using our free BMI calculator.

How to Use the BMI Calculator

Our BMI calculator helps estimate your body mass index based on your height and weight.

Steps to calculate BMI

  • Enter your weight in kilograms
  • Enter your height in centimeters
  • Click the calculate button
  • Your BMI result will appear instantly

What Your BMI Means

  • Below 18.5 – Underweight
  • 18.5 to 24.9 – Healthy weight
  • 25 to 29.9 – Overweight
  • 30 or above – Obesity
