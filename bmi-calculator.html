What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation using height and weight to estimate body fat levels.

BMI Categories

  • Below 18.5 → Underweight
  • 18.5 – 24.9 → Healthy
  • 25 – 29.9 → Overweight
  • 30+ → Obese
