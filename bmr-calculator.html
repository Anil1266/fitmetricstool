What is BMR?

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the number of calories your body needs to perform basic functions like breathing, circulation, and cell repair.

Why is BMR important?

Knowing your BMR helps you plan your diet, lose weight, or gain muscle effectively by understanding your calorie needs.
