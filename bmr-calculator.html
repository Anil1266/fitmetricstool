What is BMR?

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body needs to perform basic functions like breathing, circulation, and cell production.

Who Should Use This?

Anyone who wants to lose weight, gain muscle, or maintain health can use this tool.

Why BMR Matters

  • Shows minimum calorie needs
  • Helps create diet plans
  • Supports weight loss strategies
  • Helps track metabolism

How BMR is Calculated

This calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, one of the most accurate formulas.
