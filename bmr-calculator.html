What is BMR?

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the number of calories your body needs to maintain basic functions such as breathing, circulation and body temperature while at rest.

Why is BMR Important?

  • Helps estimate calorie needs.
  • Useful for weight loss planning.
  • Supports muscle gain calculations.
  • Used in TDEE and calorie calculators.
