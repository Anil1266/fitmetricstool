Calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate.

What is BMR?

Basal Metabolic Rate represents the number of calories your body needs to perform basic functions such as breathing and circulation.

Why BMR Matters

  • Shows minimum calorie needs
  • Helps create diet plans
  • Supports weight loss strategies
  • Helps track metabolism

How BMR is Calculated

BMR is estimated using formulas that consider age, weight, and height.
