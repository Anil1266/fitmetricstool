What is a Calorie Calculator?

The calorie calculator estimates the number of calories a person needs each day to maintain their current weight. It considers factors like age, gender, weight, height, and activity level.

Why This Calculator is Useful

  • Helps plan a healthy diet
  • Supports weight loss or muscle gain goals
  • Provides estimated daily calorie needs
  • Useful for athletes and fitness enthusiasts

How Calories Are Calculated

The calculator estimates daily energy needs using metabolic formulas combined with activity level multipliers to estimate total daily calorie expenditure.
