💪 BMR Calculator

How to Use the BMR Calculator

The BMR calculator estimates how many calories your body burns at rest. This is called your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR).

Steps to Calculate Your BMR

  • Enter your age
  • Select your gender
  • Enter your weight in kilograms
  • Enter your height in centimeters
  • Click the calculate button

Why BMR Is Important

Your BMR represents the number of calories your body needs to perform basic functions like breathing, circulation, and maintaining body temperature.

Knowing your BMR can help you plan your daily calorie intake for weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain.
