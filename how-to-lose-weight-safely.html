How to Lose Weight Safely

Losing weight safely involves creating a sustainable calorie deficit through healthy eating and regular physical activity.

Safe Weight Loss Rate

Health experts recommend losing about 0.5–1 kg per week.

Tips for Healthy Weight Loss

  • Maintain a moderate calorie deficit
  • Exercise regularly
  • Eat balanced meals with protein and vegetables
  • Stay hydrated
  • Sleep at least 7–8 hours
