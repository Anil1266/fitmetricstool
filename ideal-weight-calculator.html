Ideal Weight Calculator

What is Ideal Body Weight?

Ideal body weight refers to a healthy weight range based on height. It helps determine whether your weight is within a recommended range for overall health.

How Ideal Weight is Estimated

This calculator uses a simplified formula to estimate ideal weight based on height. It provides a general guideline and should not replace medical advice.

Why Maintaining a Healthy Weight Matters

  • Reduces risk of heart disease
  • Improves mobility and joint health
  • Supports better metabolic function
  • Boosts overall energy levels

Factors That Influence Ideal Weight

  • Age
  • Gender
  • Muscle mass
  • Body composition
  • Genetics

Healthy Weight Management Tips

  • Eat balanced meals
  • Exercise regularly
  • Stay hydrated
  • Sleep 7–8 hours daily

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ideal weight the same for everyone?

No. Ideal weight varies depending on body composition, muscle mass, and overall health.

Should I aim exactly for the calculated weight?

The value is a guideline. Focus on overall health rather than a specific number.
