Ideal Weight Calculator

What is Ideal Weight?

The Ideal Weight Calculator estimates a healthy body weight based on your height. It helps determine a target weight range for maintaining overall health.

Why Ideal Weight Matters

  • Helps maintain good health
  • Reduces risk of lifestyle diseases
  • Useful for fitness goals
  • Supports healthy body composition

How Ideal Weight is Calculated

Ideal weight formulas estimate healthy weight ranges based on height using commonly accepted medical formulas.
