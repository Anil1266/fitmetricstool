🏃 Ideal Weight Calculator

How to Use the Ideal Weight Calculator

This calculator estimates a healthy body weight range based on your height and gender. Maintaining an ideal weight can help reduce the risk of health problems and improve overall fitness.

Steps to Calculate Your Ideal Weight

  • Enter your height in centimeters
  • Select your gender
  • Click the calculate button
  • Your estimated ideal weight range will appear

Why Maintaining an Ideal Weight Is Important

Keeping your body weight within a healthy range can improve heart health, increase energy levels, and reduce the risk of conditions such as diabetes and high blood pressure.

Tips to Maintain a Healthy Weight

  • Eat balanced meals with enough nutrients
  • Exercise regularly
  • Drink enough water
  • Get enough sleep

You can also check your body mass index using our BMI Calculator.
