What is Ideal Weight?

Ideal body weight is an estimated healthy weight range based on height and gender. It can help guide fitness and health goals.

Why Ideal Weight Matters

  • Supports healthy lifestyle planning.
  • Helps track fitness goals.
  • Useful for weight management.
  • Can improve long-term health awareness.
