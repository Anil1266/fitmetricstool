Muscle Gain Nutrition Guide

Building muscle requires a combination of proper nutrition, strength training, and recovery. Consuming enough calories and protein is essential for muscle growth.

Increase Your Calorie Intake

To gain muscle, your body needs more calories than it burns. This is known as a calorie surplus.

You can estimate your calorie needs using our Calorie Calculator.

Eat Enough Protein

  • Chicken, fish, eggs
  • Beans and legumes
  • Dairy products
  • Protein shakes

Protein helps repair and grow muscle tissue after workouts.

Strength Training

Muscle growth happens when you challenge your muscles through resistance training such as weightlifting or bodyweight exercises.

Rest and Recovery

Muscles grow during recovery. Make sure to get enough sleep and allow time for muscles to recover between workouts.
