How Much Water Should You Drink Per Kg?

Water is essential for maintaining body temperature, digestion, circulation and overall health.

Water Intake Formula

A common recommendation is:

30–35 ml of water per kilogram of body weight

Example

If a person weighs 70 kg:

  • 70 × 30 ml = 2100 ml
  • 70 × 35 ml = 2450 ml
