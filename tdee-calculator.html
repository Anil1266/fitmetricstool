TDEE Calculator

Estimate your total daily energy expenditure.

What is TDEE?

Total Daily Energy Expenditure represents the total number of calories your body burns in a day including physical activity and metabolism.

Who Should Use This?

This calculator is useful for anyone who wants to monitor their health, track fitness progress, and maintain a balanced lifestyle.

Why TDEE is Important

  • Helps determine daily calorie needs
  • Supports weight loss planning
  • Useful for muscle building diets
  • Helps maintain healthy lifestyle

How TDEE is Calculated

TDEE is calculated by multiplying your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) with your activity level.
