📊 TDEE Calculator

How to Use the TDEE Calculator

The TDEE calculator estimates the total number of calories your body burns each day based on your activity level and metabolism.

Steps to Calculate Your TDEE

  • Enter your age
  • Select your gender
  • Enter your height and weight
  • Choose your daily activity level
  • Click the calculate button

What Is TDEE?

TDEE stands for Total Daily Energy Expenditure. It represents the total number of calories your body burns in a day including exercise, daily activities, and basic body functions.

Why TDEE Is Important

  • Helps determine daily calorie needs
  • Useful for weight loss planning
  • Helps with muscle gain strategies
  • Improves diet planning
