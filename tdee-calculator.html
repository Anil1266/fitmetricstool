What is TDEE?

Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) is the total number of calories your body burns in a day, including activity and metabolism.

Who Should Use This?

Anyone who wants to lose weight, gain muscle, or maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Why TDEE is Important

  • Helps determine daily calorie needs
  • Supports weight loss planning
  • Useful for muscle building diets
  • Helps maintain a healthy lifestyle

How TDEE is Calculated

TDEE is calculated by multiplying your BMR with your activity level.
