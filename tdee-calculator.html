What is TDEE?

TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) is the total number of calories your body burns daily including exercise, movement and basic body functions.

Why TDEE Matters

  • Helps estimate daily calorie needs.
  • Useful for fat loss and muscle gain goals.
  • Supports meal planning and nutrition tracking.
  • Used by athletes and fitness enthusiasts.
