What is TDEE?

Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) represents the total number of calories your body burns in a day, including physical activity and basic metabolic functions.

Why TDEE is Useful

  • Helps determine daily calorie needs
  • Useful for weight loss planning
  • Important for muscle building diets
  • Helps maintain a healthy lifestyle

How TDEE is Calculated

TDEE is calculated by multiplying your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) by an activity factor that represents your daily activity level.
