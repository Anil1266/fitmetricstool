Beginner Guide to Weight Loss

Weight loss is achieved by creating a calorie deficit, which means burning more calories than you consume. A balanced diet and regular exercise are key to sustainable weight loss.

Key Tips for Weight Loss

  • Eat a balanced diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Drink enough water
  • Get proper sleep
