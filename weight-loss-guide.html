Healthy Weight Loss Guide

Learn sustainable and healthy ways to lose weight using proper nutrition, calorie management and exercise.

What Causes Weight Loss?

Weight loss happens when your body burns more calories than it consumes. This is called a calorie deficit.

  • Eating fewer calories
  • Increasing physical activity
  • Improving nutrition quality
  • Consistent healthy habits

Best Strategies for Healthy Weight Loss

  • Focus on whole foods
  • Increase protein intake
  • Exercise regularly
  • Drink enough water
  • Sleep properly
Pro Tip: Sustainable weight loss is usually around 0.5–1 kg per week.

Importance of Calorie Tracking

Tracking calories helps you understand your eating habits and maintain a healthy calorie deficit.

Use calculators to estimate your calorie needs accurately.

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast should I lose weight?

A healthy and sustainable rate is usually 0.5–1 kg per week.

Is cardio necessary for weight loss?

Cardio helps burn calories, but nutrition and calorie balance are most important.

Do I need to avoid carbs?

No. Balanced nutrition is more important than removing entire food groups.
