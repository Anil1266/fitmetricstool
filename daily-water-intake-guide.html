How Much Water Should You Drink Per Day?

Water is essential for maintaining overall health and proper body function. Every cell, tissue, and organ in your body needs water to work correctly.

Why Hydration Is Important

Drinking enough water helps regulate body temperature, supports digestion, improves energy levels, and helps remove waste from the body.

Recommended Daily Water Intake

A general guideline is to drink about:

  • 3.7 liters per day for men
  • 2.7 liters per day for women

However, the exact amount depends on your body weight, activity level, climate, and overall health.

Signs You May Not Be Drinking Enough Water

  • Dry mouth
  • Fatigue
  • Headaches
  • Dark yellow urine
