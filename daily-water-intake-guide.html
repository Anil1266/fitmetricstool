Daily Water Intake Guide

Learn how hydration affects energy, fitness, recovery and overall health while understanding how much water your body needs daily.

Why Hydration Matters

Water supports essential body functions including temperature regulation, digestion, circulation and physical performance.

Proper hydration also helps maintain energy levels, concentration and recovery after exercise.

How Much Water Should You Drink?

Water requirements vary based on:

  • Body weight
  • Physical activity
  • Climate and temperature
  • Diet and lifestyle

Many people benefit from approximately 2–4 litres of water daily depending on activity levels.

Signs of Dehydration

  • Fatigue or low energy
  • Dry mouth
  • Dark yellow urine
  • Headaches
  • Reduced exercise performance

Hydration Tips

  • Carry a water bottle daily.
  • Drink water consistently throughout the day.
  • Increase intake during workouts.
  • Consume water-rich foods.
  • Track hydration habits regularly.
