How Many Calories Should You Eat Per Day?

Calories are the units of energy your body uses to perform daily activities such as breathing, walking, and digestion. Your daily calorie needs depend on factors such as age, gender, body weight, height, and physical activity level.

Average Daily Calorie Needs

  • Adult women: 1,800 – 2,200 calories
  • Adult men: 2,200 – 2,800 calories

Calories for Weight Loss

To lose weight safely, most people reduce their daily calorie intake by about 300–500 calories below maintenance level.

Calories for Weight Gain

To gain weight, you generally need to consume about 300–500 extra calories per day.
